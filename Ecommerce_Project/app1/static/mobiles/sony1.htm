Smartphone

A smartphone is a cell phone with a built-in computer and other functions that were not originally connected to phones, such as the operating system, web browsing and the ability to run software applications.

What a smartphone can do?

In general, the smartphone will be based on the operating system, which allows you to run applications. Apple’s iPhone uses iOS, and BlackBerry smartphones work with the BlackBerry operating system. Other devices work with the Android OS from Google, webOS and Microsoft Windows Phone.

Why is it important to have a Smartphone?

Smartphones are so important today because of their connectivity. One of the most popular applications for smartphones is Instant Messaging from Whatsapp. It works on an internet connection and can use Wi-Fi to make sure it does not exist in your data plan.

Why Mobile was invented?

The first mobile phone invented for practical use was by a Motorola employee named Martin Cooper, who is considered a key player in the history of mobile phones. Cooper made the history of mobile phones in April 1973, when he made his first conversation with a portable mobile phone.

Why do we use a Mobile Phone?

That’s why mobile communication is sometimes called cellular communication. After connecting to the station, the mobile phone can make calls. Since the network knows that the phone is connected to this particular cell, it can also make calls to the mobile phone.
